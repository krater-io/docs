sidebarTitle: "Deploy Smart-Wallet"
title: "Setting Up Your Smart Wallet"
---

1. Visit the Krater Dashboard and click on the "Balance" button to be redirected to the wallet 
set up page.
    
    <Frame caption="Click on the “Balance” button."><img height="200" src="https://file.notion.so/f/s/938dfbc8-df8c-44b5-9670-92ca7515378c/Untitled.png?id=ca390c53-0c36-4997-b16e-0b456996cc32&table=block&spaceId=d943086f-50b5-4b4d-99ed-11c46e374e94&expirationTimestamp=1689127200000&signature=QxdGFVFOL45odmsg4zwVDxna__iUyzl3_UjoawZOulw&downloadName=Untitled.png" /></Frame>
    
2. You will be prompted to set up your personal "Smart Wallet." This smart contract-based wallet is designed for interacting with Krater's platform.
    
    <Frame caption="Click on the “Connect Wallet” button, make sure you have Metamask Wallet extension."><img height="200" src="https://file.notion.so/f/s/89d2b305-15ce-433d-8182-094303cea828/Untitled.png?id=04d3a3bf-03a8-4e1f-93b7-8b01512eab88&table=block&spaceId=d943086f-50b5-4b4d-99ed-11c46e374e94&expirationTimestamp=1689127200000&signature=ETnqUM-DOvTFgkOuYPJ5ZcCUqZBrhFNBgv1WUk6ykFU&downloadName=Untitled.png" /></Frame>
        
3. Connect your preferred Ethereum wallet (at this time only Metamask is supported) to your Krater account. This will enable you to access and manage your smart wallet seamlessly.
    
    <Frame caption="Select an account in Metamask and press “Next”."><img height="200" src="https://file.notion.so/f/s/bf693013-ac0a-4af9-93f2-b2725285a852/Untitled.png?id=7f1f30ef-1bbd-41ed-872a-df0ae1c05b01&table=block&spaceId=d943086f-50b5-4b4d-99ed-11c46e374e94&expirationTimestamp=1689127200000&signature=8dMaU6cSGMeCh8ndtY5yHqLDSEFHoOWkDVe6u1NIOns&downloadName=Untitled.png" /></Frame>
    
4. Before you can proceed with creating the smart wallet, you will need to switch to the Polygon Mainnet Network.
    
    ![Switch network to “Polygon Mainnet”.
    ](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/secure.notion-static.com/84301b93-125a-40b9-b095-97d616c474c9/Untitled.png)
    
    Switch network to “Polygon Mainnet”.
    
5. After the Polygon Network was added to your Metamask and selected you will be able to click on “Create Smart-Wallet” button.
    
    ![Press “Create Smart Wallet”.
    ](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/secure.notion-static.com/569a694b-9490-425b-b4df-eca3ab5d04a6/Untitled.png)
    
    Press “Create Smart Wallet”.
    
6. To create the wallet, you will need to provide your signature. This transaction occurs on the blockchain, but we subsidize the transactions for our users so that they do not have to pay for gas fees. Your signature will be sufficient.
    
    ![Metamask extension will pop up and ask for a signature, click “Sign” to proceed.
    ](https://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/secure.notion-static.com/8c6eb27e-0448-4042-a708-e3048079e37b/Untitled.png)
    
    Metamask extension will pop up and ask for a signature, click “Sign” to proceed.
    
7. Congratulations! Your Smart-Wallet has been created and deployed to the Polygon Blockchain. You can now use your wallet to start interacting with Krater.
